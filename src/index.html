01/ Clique

MongoDB | Express | React | Node

Clique is a social chat application that visualizes users as hexagonal tokens and chat spaces as slots than they can fit into. Users can chat with other people in their channel (clique), search for other cliques, or create their own. Interacting with other users in a clique makes it grow in size.

This app is a work-in-progress.
